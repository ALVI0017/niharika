Collaboration With Artist Jamal Ahmed:

News Link

Niharika Momtaz blends visual art with Fashion Art isn't just for walls anymore, Niharika Momtaz comes up with her new bespoke collection. This time Niharika Momtaz, is preparing to welcome audiences with a completely different production. Dubai based Bangladeshi Designer, Niharika Momtaz and eminent artist Jamal Ahmed collaborated and stunningly blends art with fashion for the first time ever! She decided to unleash the visual artist’s creativity,and arts from inside the walls of the cold galleries to scatter spectra colors on her Saree in a charming beauty. Artistic creativity is a source of pleasure for eye and soul. It is priceless. When art and creativity blend into life,beauty and joy increase. But the step of mixing art with fashion is an important move that has caused a boom in public taste. For those who wear a fashion piece signed by a visual artist,the difficulty lies in the level of glamor,elegance and pride of what they wear as it is not easy for them to be a part of an artistic work. A collection is being ready for an international fashion show. The art work will be displayed at the international platform to highlight the Art work in textiles. To promote Bangladeshi art work and the fine quality silk saree.

Saree : Tassar Silk | Made In Bangladesh The sarees are signed by the artist himself.
