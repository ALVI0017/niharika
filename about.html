Niharika Momtaz

From the the Haute Couture fashion world of Dubai—Niharika Momtaz’s rise as an entrepreneur, art lover, fashion and Jewelery designer is a story befitting a fairytale.

Hailing from an upscale neighborhood of Chittagong, Bangladesh Niharika stepped into the domain of clothing design at the very early age of 18! The stint was brief and after having veered into the world of corporate finance for a decade, she has once again resumed her passionate path by designing jewelry and fashion labels. Came year 2012 and her jewelry labels Outliner and 1972 Couture Jewelery were launched from this inspiration. Point to note that as a profession she did her Chartered accountancy Articleship with KPMG Bangladesh. She worked for an NGO called ‘Acid Survivors Foundation’ As Head Of Finance & Find Raiser from 2000 to 2008.

Niharika has showcased her Brandz at various international fashion shows in London, Colombo, Cannes, Singapore, Dubai, Doha, Dhaka and Chittagong. She has just showcased her Brandz in Cannes during 71st & 72nd Cannes Film Festival in May representing Bangladesh at International platform. Moreover, she was one of the Jury and the official Fashion consultant for “Miss Universal 2018” held in Turkey. She also has arranged a number of photo exhibitions, art exhibition and fashion shows in different capacities.

Her gorgeous glamazon works amplifies woman’s individual style quotient. She believes “WORK HARD IN SILENCE, LET YOUR SUCCESS BE YOUR NOISE”.

...

She was always keen to express her designing skills and interest in art. Her another endeavour is to promote art & culture of Bangladesh. Hence, Niharika Momtaz initiated and created a platform called Bangladesh Art Week ( BAW) for Creatives on the ground and outside of the institution - artists across the spectrum of visual arts, performing arts, sculptural installations, architectural installations, photography, videography, music, deaign etc to the world.

“Bangladesh Art Week (BAW), is the voice of Bangladesh’s art industries. To creat a more diverse artistic environment and to promote art appreciation worldwide.”

Her new endeavour is to promote heritage & culture of Bangladesh. Hence, Niharika Momtaz took the initiative to create a platform called Heritage Bangladesh Foundation ( HBF). Heritage Bangladesh Foundation is the voice of heritages of Bangladesh. It's objectives are-to carry out or engage in philanthropic activities, conservation, preservation, protection, promotion of heritage sites, monuments , buildings, heritage textiles, crafts and cultural heritage of Bangladesh.

For Niharika it has been instrumental in making women all over Bangladesh aware of not just their rights, but their potential too in all spheres of life. She wants to be a contemporary and thinking woman’s guide to stay on top whether she is from any walk of life.
